# Background music — Freddi Fish intro

The site’s **🎵** button plays the main title from *Freddi Fish and the Case of the Missing Kelp Seeds* (“Introduction” / “Mr. Two Notes (Title)”), composed by George “The Fat Man” Sanger and Team Fat.

## Recommended: local file (best quality, works offline)

1. Purchase or rip the track you own from the [remastered Bandcamp soundtrack](https://thefatmanandteamfat.bandcamp.com/album/freddi-fish-and-the-case-of-the-missing-kelp-seeds-remastered-soundtrack) or your game files.
2. Save it as:

   `audio/freddi-intro.mp3`

3. Reload the site and turn on background music with **🎵**.

Supported name: `freddi-intro.mp3` only (path is fixed in `index.html`).

## Fallback: YouTube stream

If `freddi-intro.mp3` is missing, the player uses a no-cookie embed of [this intro upload](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=tPsoMseeZ-g). That needs network access and may be blocked by some browsers until you click anywhere on the page.

## Legal note

Do not commit copyrighted audio to a public repo unless you have the rights. Host the MP3 yourself or keep it local for development.
